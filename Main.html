What Kind of Game Should You Play?

Read the summaries of the genres below and select which type of game most interests you.

Action

The action genre focuses on fast paced combat as its core gameplay. Expect games that focus more on fighting than story and often have relatively easy to pick up gameplay mechanics to keep things moving smoothly.

Visit the Action Page
Puzzle

The puzzle genre focuses primarily on solving logical problems in a fun and simple format. Puzzle games are usually better played in short bursts and can be quite addicting. From moving blocks around to solving word problems, This genre will test your brain.

Visit the Puzzle Page
Role-Playing Game

The RPG, or Role-Playing game, is a genre that puts you in the shoes of a character that sets out on a story driven quest to accomplish a goal. Generally slower paced and featuring characters that grow steadily stronger over the course of the game the RPG focuses on story above all else. This is a genre where your choices can effect how the story plays out and the characters develop leading to potentially having multiple stories in a single game.
