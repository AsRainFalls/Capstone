    Tetris

    Tetris is the original block puzzle game that is easy to learn but hard to master. Moving falling 2D blacks left or right and rotating them is the only thing that you can do and your goal is to make a horizontal line of blocks that spans the ful screen from left to right. Tetris is fun for all ages and can be played for 5 minutes or 5 hours with equal enjoyment.

    Bejeweled

    Bejeweled is one of the most played games in history and many similar games draw inspiration from the free to play classic. A two dimension grid of colored jewels appears on screen and the goal is to swap two touching jewels to make lines of same color jewels that will dissapear and give you points. Great for people that don't like to feel pressured by time limits or competition.

    Words with Friends

    Words with friends is a competitive puzzle game based on scrabble. It uses mobile phaones or tablets to allow people to play against each other regardless of distance. great for people looking for some competition.
