    Devil May Cry

    DMC, or Devil May Cry, is a an action-platformer/hack-and-slash game that focuses on stylish combat and fluid combos. The geame centers on main character Dante as he works as a demon-hunter for hire in the modern world. DMC is known for having high levels of difficulty and may not be kind to new players.

    Insurgency

    Insurgency is a hyper realistic First-Person-Shooter (FPS) that was created by veterans. it features extreme levels of realism in weapon handing and gameplay mechanics to the point where you need to research your weapons to even know how much ammo it carries. This game rewards careful, strategic gameplay and close teamwork.

    Splatoon

    Splatoon is a third-person-shooter (TPS) that focuses on child friendly gameplay following a group of squid-people who are fighting over territory through a form of very aggressive paintball. Fast paced and fun for all ages this game has a strong arcade-like feel to it.
