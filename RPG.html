    8. Role-Playing games

    The Legend of Zelda

    The legend of zelda is an open world action-RPG with a focus on exploration and puzzle solving. The game follows a typical fantasy setting of following a hero as he grows stronger over the course of his journey to ultimately take down a great evil.

    Final Fantasy

    The final fantasy series is a more traditional JRPG (Japanese RPG) that typically focuses on turn based combat and a heavy focus on character stats and equipment. Stories range from high fantasy settings to sci-fi blends but generally feature a team of heroes rising up against a great evil.

    Pokemon

    The pokemon series is a an excellent place to start for both new gamers and children. Desinged to be child friendly but often possessing deep stories the Pokemon series is a turn based stat based RPG with a focus on team building and taking advantage of the various strengths and weaknesses of the various creatures within.
